Our Mission

At PetalPress, we believe in the transformative power of flowers. Our mission is to cultivate inspiration, nurture creativity, and blossom connections between people and the natural world. Through our carefully curated content, we aim to demystify floral design and gardening, making it accessible to enthusiasts of all skill levels.

We’re committed to sustainable practices, seasonal awareness, and celebrating the unique beauty of each bloom. Whether you’re a seasoned gardener or just discovering your green thumb, we’re here to guide you through every step of your floral journey.

Our Story

2010

The Seed is Planted

Sarah starts a small flower blog documenting her journey from corporate life to floristry school, sharing tips and discoveries with a growing audience of flower enthusiasts.

2013

First Physical Workshop

Hosting our first in-person floral arrangement workshop in Sarah's backyard marks the transition from digital to physical community building.

2015

PetalPress is Born

The blog officially becomes PetalPress with a complete redesign and expanded team, including Michael joining to lead gardening content.

2018

Sustainable Practices Initiative

We launch our commitment to sustainable floristry, partnering with local growers and pioneering eco-friendly floral design techniques.

2021

Community Garden Project

Opening our first community garden space where workshops, photoshoots, and community events bring our online community together in person.

2023

Global Recognition

PetalPress is recognized by the International Floral Association for excellence in floral education and innovation in digital content.
